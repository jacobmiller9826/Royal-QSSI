Security: The Inner Spiral

Purpose: Filter threats, protect capital, maintain control.

  • Bone Principle: Signal opportunities (events, tours, data drops).
  • Observation: Track competitor reactions.
  • Center Maintenance: Keep capital and operations insulated.
  • Exit Strategy: Predefine triggers for decisive action.

Checklist:

  • Identify threats & opportunities
  • Deploy controlled “bone”
  • Observe competitor behavior
  • Maintain central control
  • Evaluate exit triggers
  • Execute exit when conditions are met
